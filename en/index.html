Welcome home

On the banks of Lake Ram, in the village of Mas'ade in the northern Golan Heights, a warm stone house awaits — with a blooming garden, cherry trees and open views of Mount Hermon. Omar and Suleiman host you like family: coffee on the terrace, a wood fire in winter, and breakfast from the local kitchen.

  • 15 min from the Hermon ski site
  • Local breakfast fresh every morning
  • Free parking
  • Wood fireplace in every unit
